Best Resume Formats for 2025 (With Examples)

Intro:
The right resume format helps you present your strengths and make a great first impression. In 2025, formatting matters more than ever — for both humans and hiring bots.

1. Chronological Format (Most Common)

  • Best for: steady career paths, traditional industries
  • Structure: Work experience in reverse chronological order
  • Strength: Shows career progression clearly

2. Functional Format

  • Best for: career changers, gaps in work history
  • Structure: Skills and accomplishments first, experience last
  • Strength: De-emphasizes job titles, focuses on transferable skills

3. Hybrid Format

  • Best for: most modern professionals
  • Structure: Combines summary, key skills, and chronological experience
  • Strength: Balances skills with experience, great for versatile roles

What to Avoid:
❌ Creative resumes with heavy visuals, columns, or embedded images
❌ Unconventional headings or file types (e.g. .pages, .txt)

Tips:

  • Use consistent formatting for dates and locations
  • Use bullet points, not paragraphs
  • Stick to fonts like Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman
